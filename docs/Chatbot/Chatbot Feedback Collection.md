# Chatbot Feedback Collection

Enable users to rate chatbot responses and collect feedback to improve your knowledge base and bot accuracy.

---

## Why Collect Feedback?

User feedback helps you:
- Identify answers that are unhelpful or inaccurate.
- Discover content gaps.
- Measure overall chatbot satisfaction.

---

## Enabling Feedback

1. Go to **Settings → Chatbot → Feedback**.
2. Toggle **Enable Feedback** to ON.
3. Choose the feedback type:
   - **Thumbs up / Thumbs down**
   - **Star rating (1–5)**
4. Optionally enable a **Comment Box** to collect free-text feedback.
5. Save your settings.

---

## Feedback Widget

After each bot response, users will see the feedback prompt:

> "Was this answer helpful?"  👍 👎

If a comment box is enabled, a text field appears when the user clicks 👎.

---

## Viewing Feedback Data

1. Navigate to **Analytics → Chatbot → Feedback**.
2. Review:
   - Positive vs. negative ratings.
   - User comments.
   - Articles associated with low-rated answers.

---

## Acting on Feedback

- **Negative responses** – Review the associated article and improve its content.
- **Repeated negative feedback** – Consider restructuring the topic or adding a dedicated FAQ.
- **Positive responses** – Use as a baseline to identify what good answers look like.

---

## Summary

Collecting and acting on feedback creates a continuous improvement loop that keeps your chatbot and documentation performing at their best.
