# Chatbot Overview

Document360's Chatbot is an AI-powered assistant that helps users find answers instantly from your knowledge base without browsing through articles manually.

---

## What Is the Document360 Chatbot?

The Document360 Chatbot uses artificial intelligence to understand user questions and deliver accurate, context-aware responses drawn from your published documentation.

---

## Key Benefits

- **Instant answers** – Users get responses in seconds.
- **Reduced support tickets** – Deflect common queries automatically.
- **Always available** – 24/7 availability without human intervention.
- **Contextual responses** – Answers are based on your actual documentation.

---

## How It Works

1. A user types a question into the chatbot widget.
2. The AI processes the query and searches the knowledge base.
3. The most relevant answer is returned along with links to related articles.

---

## Summary

The Document360 Chatbot transforms your knowledge base into an interactive self-service tool, improving user experience and reducing support overhead.
