# Chatbot for Customer Support Teams

Learn how support teams can leverage the Document360 Chatbot to reduce ticket volume and improve response times.

---

## The Challenge

Support teams often handle a high volume of repetitive questions that could be resolved through self-service. The Document360 Chatbot addresses this by giving users instant answers before they reach out to a human agent.

---

## Benefits for Support Teams

- **Reduced ticket volume** – Deflect up to 40% of common queries.
- **Faster resolution** – Users get answers in seconds, not hours.
- **24/7 coverage** – Bot handles queries outside business hours.
- **Focus on complex issues** – Agents spend time on problems that need human judgment.

## Escalation Workflow

When the chatbot cannot resolve a query:

1. User is shown an escalation message.
2. A support ticket is created automatically.
3. The full conversation transcript is attached to the ticket.
4. A support agent picks up the ticket with full context.

---

## Monitoring for Support Managers

Support managers can review:
- **Escalation rate trends** over time.
- **Top escalated topics** to identify documentation gaps.
- **Resolution rate** by category.

---

## Summary

Integrating the Document360 Chatbot with your support workflow creates an efficient tiered support system that benefits both users and agents.
