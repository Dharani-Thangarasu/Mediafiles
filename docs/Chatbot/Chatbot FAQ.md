# Chatbot FAQ

Answers to common questions about the Document360 Chatbot feature.

---

## General Questions

### What is the Document360 Chatbot?
The Document360 Chatbot is an AI-powered assistant embedded in your knowledge base portal. It answers user questions by searching your published articles.

### Which pricing plans include the Chatbot?
The Chatbot is available on **Business** and **Enterprise** plans. Check the [Pricing page](https://document360.com/pricing/) for details.

### Does the Chatbot support multiple languages?
Yes. The Chatbot can serve responses in any language supported by your knowledge base.

---

## Setup Questions

### How long does setup take?
Initial setup takes approximately 5–10 minutes. Training the bot on your content may take a few additional minutes depending on the size of your knowledge base.

### Can I restrict which articles the bot uses?
Yes. You can limit the bot to specific categories, tags, or article versions in the configuration settings.

---

## Performance Questions

### Why is the bot giving incorrect answers?
This usually happens when articles are outdated or lack clear headings and keywords. Review the **Unanswered Questions** report in Analytics and update your content accordingly.

### How often does the bot re-index my content?
The bot automatically re-indexes whenever an article is published or updated. You can also trigger a manual re-train from **Settings → Chatbot → Training**.

---

## Security Questions

### Is user conversation data stored?
Conversation logs are stored securely and accessible only to project admins. Data retention policies follow Document360's standard privacy terms.
