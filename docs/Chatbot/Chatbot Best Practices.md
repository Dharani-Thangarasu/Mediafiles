# Chatbot Best Practices

Follow these guidelines to get the best results from your Document360 Chatbot.

---

## 1. Keep Articles Focused

Each article should cover a single topic or task. Avoid combining unrelated subjects in one article, as this reduces the bot's ability to return precise answers.

---

## 2. Use Clear Headings

Structure articles with descriptive H2 and H3 headings. The chatbot uses headings to identify the most relevant sections to surface.

---

## 3. Write in Natural Language

Write content the way your users speak. Include phrases like "How do I…", "What is…", and "Why does…" to match common query patterns.

---

## 4. Add FAQ Sections

Place a FAQ section at the bottom of key articles. These directly mirror the questions users ask and improve match accuracy significantly.

---

## 5. Maintain Up-to-Date Content

Outdated content leads to wrong answers. Set a review schedule for articles and archive deprecated topics promptly.

---

## 6. Monitor Unanswered Questions

Regularly review the **Unanswered Questions** report in Analytics to discover knowledge gaps and create new content.

---

## 7. Use Synonyms and Variants

Include alternate terms users might use. For example, if your product calls something "workspace," also mention "project" or "environment" if users use those terms.

---

## 8. Set a Realistic Confidence Threshold

Start with a confidence threshold of 60–70% and adjust based on user feedback and resolved rate metrics.

---

## Summary

A well-maintained knowledge base is the foundation of a high-performing chatbot. Consistent content hygiene and analytics-driven improvements will keep your chatbot accurate and helpful.
