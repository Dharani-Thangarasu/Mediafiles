# Chatbot Branding and White-Labeling

Customize the chatbot's visual identity to align with your brand, including logo, colors, and bot name.

---

## Why Brand Your Chatbot?

A branded chatbot:
- Creates a seamless experience consistent with your product.
- Builds user trust by matching your visual identity.
- Reinforces your brand even in automated interactions.

---

## Available Branding Options

| Option              | Description                                              |
|---------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| **Bot Name**        | Display name shown in the chat header.                   |
| **Bot Avatar**      | Upload a custom icon (PNG or SVG, 64×64 px recommended). |
| **Primary Color**   | Header and button color.                                 |
| **Background Color**| Chat window background.                                  |
| **Font**            | Match the widget font to your site typography.           |
| **Widget Icon**     | Custom icon for the collapsed chat bubble.               |

---

## Removing "Powered by Document360"

Enterprise plan customers can remove the **"Powered by Document360"** branding from the chat widget.

To enable:
1. Go to **Settings → Chatbot → Branding**.
2. Toggle **Remove Powered by Document360** to ON.
3. Save changes.

---

## Custom Domain

Enterprise customers can serve the chatbot widget from a custom subdomain (e.g., `chat.yourbrand.com`) for full white-label control.

---

## Logo Usage Guidelines

- Use a high-contrast image that is clearly visible against the chosen primary color.
- Avoid transparent backgrounds if the icon blends into the header.
- Test on both desktop and mobile views before publishing.

---

## Summary

Full branding and white-label support on Enterprise plans ensures the chatbot feels like a native part of your product rather than a third-party tool.
