# Setting Up the Chatbot

This article walks you through the initial setup of the Document360 Chatbot for your knowledge base project.

---

## Prerequisites

- An active Document360 account with a Business or Enterprise plan.
- At least one published article in your knowledge base.
- Admin or Owner role in your project.

---

## Steps to Set Up

### 1. Navigate to Chatbot Settings

Go to **Settings → Chatbot** in your Document360 project dashboard.

### 2. Enable the Chatbot

Toggle the **Enable Chatbot** switch to turn on the feature.

### 3. Configure Basic Options

- **Bot Name** – Give your chatbot a friendly name (e.g., "Aria").
- **Welcome Message** – Set the greeting users see when they open the chat.
- **Fallback Message** – Define what the bot says when it cannot find an answer.

### 4. Select Knowledge Base Sources

Choose which categories or sections of your knowledge base the chatbot should draw answers from.

### 5. Save and Publish

Click **Save** to apply your settings. The chatbot will be live on your knowledge base portal immediately.

---

## Summary

Setting up the chatbot takes only a few minutes. Once enabled, it begins serving answers to your readers automatically.
