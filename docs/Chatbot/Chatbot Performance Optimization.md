# Chatbot Performance Optimization

Tips and strategies to maximize the speed, accuracy, and reliability of your Document360 Chatbot.

---

## 1. Optimize Your Knowledge Base Structure

A well-organized knowledge base directly improves chatbot performance.

- Use clear, concise article titles.
- Keep articles under 2,000 words where possible.
- Break long articles into multiple focused pages.
- Use H2 and H3 headings to organize content logically.

---

## 2. Minimize Low-Quality Articles

Articles that are incomplete, outdated, or off-topic can degrade bot accuracy. Regularly audit your content using:

- **Article Health Reports** in Document360 Analytics.
- Review dates and expiry flags for time-sensitive content.

---

## 3. Tune the Confidence Threshold

The confidence threshold determines when the bot returns an answer vs. a fallback message.

| Threshold Setting | Effect                                     |
|-------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| Too Low (<50%)    | Bot returns inaccurate or irrelevant answers. |
| Optimal (60–75%)  | Balance between answer rate and accuracy.  |
| Too High (>85%)   | Bot frequently returns fallback messages.  |

---

## 4. Use Tags and Categories Effectively

Tag articles with relevant keywords and assign them to appropriate categories. The chatbot uses this metadata to narrow search results.

---

## 5. Monitor and Iterate

- Review **Analytics → Chatbot** weekly.
- Use the **Unanswered Questions** report to identify gaps.
- Re-train the bot after publishing new content.

---

## 6. Leverage FAQ Sections

Adding dedicated FAQ sections to articles significantly improves the bot's ability to match natural language queries.

---

## Summary

Chatbot performance is directly tied to knowledge base quality. Continuous content improvement, combined with smart configuration, delivers the best user outcomes.
