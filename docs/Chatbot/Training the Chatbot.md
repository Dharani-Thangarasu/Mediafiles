# Training the Chatbot

Improve the accuracy and relevance of your chatbot's responses by training it with your knowledge base content.

---

## How Training Works

The Document360 Chatbot is powered by AI that indexes your published articles. Training involves:

1. **Indexing** – The bot crawls your published articles and builds a search index.
2. **Learning** – Natural language processing (NLP) maps user queries to relevant content.
3. **Updating** – Every time you publish or update an article, the index refreshes automatically.

---

## Manual Re-Training

If you want to force a re-index after bulk changes:

1. Go to **Settings → Chatbot → Training**.
2. Click **Re-train Now**.
3. Wait for the process to complete (usually a few minutes).

---

## Best Practices for Better Training

- **Use clear, descriptive headings** in your articles.
- **Add FAQs** at the end of each article.
- **Include keywords** users are likely to type.
- **Keep articles focused** on a single topic.
- **Use consistent terminology** across your knowledge base.

---

## Training Status

The Training page shows:
- Last trained date and time.
- Number of articles indexed.
- Any indexing errors.

---

## Summary

Regular training and well-structured content are the keys to a highly accurate chatbot that delights your users.
