---

## Monthly Query Limits

| Plan       | Queries per Month |
|------------|-------------------|
| Business   | 10,000            |
| Enterprise | Unlimited         |

---

## Overage Policy

If you exceed your monthly query limit on the Business plan:
- Additional queries are billed at a per-query rate.
- You will receive an email notification when you reach 80% of your limit.

---

## Upgrading Your Plan

To unlock Chatbot features:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**.
3. Select **Business** or **Enterprise**.
4. Complete payment.

---

## Summary

The Document360 Chatbot is available on Business and Enterprise plans, with Enterprise offering unlimited queries, extended data retention, and advanced security options.
