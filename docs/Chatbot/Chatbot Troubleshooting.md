# Chatbot Troubleshooting

Find solutions to common issues with the Document360 Chatbot.

---

## Issue: Chatbot Is Not Appearing on the Portal

**Possible Causes & Solutions:**

1. **Chatbot is disabled** – Go to **Settings → Chatbot** and ensure the toggle is ON.
2. **Embed code missing** – If using a custom website, verify the embed script is added correctly before the `</body>` tag.
3. **Browser cache** – Clear your browser cache and reload the page.
4. **Ad blocker** – Disable ad blockers or whitelist your domain.

---

## Issue: Bot Is Giving Incorrect Answers

**Possible Causes & Solutions:**

1. **Outdated content** – Ensure all articles are up-to-date and republished.
2. **Low confidence threshold** – Increase the threshold in **Settings → Chatbot → Configuration**.
3. **Missing content** – Create articles for topics the bot is failing to answer.
4. **Re-train the bot** – Go to **Settings → Chatbot → Training** and click **Re-train Now**.

---

## Issue: Bot Shows "I Don't Know" Frequently

This is usually caused by content gaps. Steps to resolve:

1. Review the **Unanswered Questions** report.
2. Create or update articles for frequently asked but unanswered queries.
3. Add FAQ sections to existing articles.

---

## Issue: Escalation Is Not Working

1. Verify escalation is enabled in **Settings → Chatbot → Escalation**.
2. Check the escalation channel configuration (e.g., webhook URL, email address).
3. Test the escalation trigger manually.

---

## Issue: Analytics Data Is Not Loading

1. Ensure you have **Admin** or **Owner** role access.
2. Try a different browser.
3. Contact Document360 Support if the issue persists.

---

## Summary

Most chatbot issues can be resolved through settings verification, content updates, or a manual re-train. For persistent issues, contact [Document360 Support](https://support.document360.com).
