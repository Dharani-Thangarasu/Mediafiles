# Chatbot Conversation History

Document360 stores chatbot conversation logs so that admins can review interactions, identify issues, and improve the knowledge base.

---

## Accessing Conversation History

1. Navigate to **Analytics → Chatbot → Conversations**.
2. View a list of all past chat sessions sorted by date.

---

## Conversation Details

Each conversation record includes:

| Field             | Description                                          |
|-------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| **Session ID**    | Unique identifier for the conversation.              |
| **Date & Time**   | When the conversation took place.                    |
| **User Query**    | The exact question the user asked.                   |
| **Bot Response**  | The answer provided by the chatbot.                  |
| **Confidence**    | The confidence score of the matched answer.          |
| **Resolved**      | Whether the user's query was resolved.               |
| **Escalated**     | Whether the conversation was escalated to support.   |

---

## Filtering Conversations

Use filters to narrow results:
- **Date Range**
- **Resolved / Unresolved**
- **Escalated**
- **Keyword search**

---

## Data Retention

Conversation history is retained according to your plan's data retention policy. Enterprise plans support extended retention periods.

---

## Exporting History

Click **Export CSV** to download conversation data for external analysis or compliance reporting.

---

## Summary

Conversation history is a valuable resource for understanding what your users need and continuously improving both your chatbot and your documentation.
