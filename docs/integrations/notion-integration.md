# Notion Integration

Sync Document360 with Notion to bring your knowledge base content into collaborative workspaces and vice versa.

---

## Overview

The Notion integration lets teams import content from Notion pages into Document360 or link Notion databases to documentation workflows, reducing duplication and keeping information consistent.

---

## Prerequisites

- A Notion workspace with admin permissions
- Document360 project admin access
- Notion API integration token

---

## Setup Steps

1. Navigate to **Settings → Integrations → Notion** in Document360.
2. Click **Connect with Notion**.
3. Authorize Document360 to access your Notion workspace.
4. Select the Notion pages or databases to link.
5. Configure sync direction (Notion → Document360 or bidirectional).
6. Click **Save**.

---

## Features

- **Content Import** – Import Notion pages as Document360 articles in one click.
- **Two-Way Sync** – Keep content updated in both platforms automatically.
- **Database Linking** – Link Notion databases to Documentation categories.
- **Real-Time Updates** – Reflect Notion page edits in Document360 automatically.

---

## Use Cases

- Migrate internal Notion wikis to a customer-facing Document360 knowledge base.
- Use Notion for drafting and Document360 for publishing.
- Share a single source of truth across both platforms.

---

## Best Practices

- Organize Notion pages with clear hierarchies before syncing to Document360.
- Review imported content for formatting consistency after migration.
- Use Notion's page permissions to control what gets synced.

---

## Summary

The Notion integration makes it easy to bridge internal wikis with your customer-facing knowledge base, ensuring consistent and up-to-date documentation across both platforms.
