# Freshdesk Integration

Integrate Document360 with Freshdesk to bring knowledge base articles directly into your customer support workflow.

---

## Overview

The Freshdesk integration allows support agents to search and share Document360 articles from within Freshdesk tickets, reducing resolution time and improving customer satisfaction.

---

## Prerequisites

- A Freshdesk account with admin access
- Document360 project admin access
- Document360 API key

---

## Setup Steps

1. In Document360, navigate to **Settings → API** and generate an **API Key**.
2. Open your Freshdesk account and go to **Admin → Apps → Get More Apps**.
3. Search for and install the **Document360 app**.
4. Enter your Document360 **API Key** and **Project URL**.
5. Click **Save**.

---

## Features

- **Article Search Panel** – Search the knowledge base from within any Freshdesk ticket.
- **One-Click Insert** – Insert article links directly into ticket replies.
- **Auto-Suggestions** – Get article suggestions based on ticket subject and description.
- **Deflection Tracking** – Measure how often knowledge base articles resolve tickets.

---

## Use Cases

- Empower agents to resolve tickets faster using up-to-date documentation.
- Reduce ticket volume by enabling self-service through embedded knowledge base links.
- Identify frequently referenced articles and keep them updated.

---

## Best Practices

- Train support agents to use the Document360 search panel regularly.
- Use Freshdesk ticket tags to identify which topics need better documentation coverage.
- Monitor article engagement from Freshdesk to prioritize documentation updates.

---

## Summary

The Freshdesk integration seamlessly connects your knowledge base with your helpdesk, making support operations more efficient and customer experiences more consistent.
