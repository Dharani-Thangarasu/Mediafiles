# HubSpot Integration

Connect Document360 with HubSpot to align your documentation with your marketing and CRM workflows.

---

## Overview

The HubSpot integration lets you embed knowledge base content in HubSpot campaigns, track documentation engagement alongside CRM data, and power self-service experiences for leads and customers.

---

## Prerequisites

- A HubSpot account (Marketing Hub or Service Hub recommended)
- Document360 project admin access
- HubSpot API key or OAuth connection

---

## Setup Steps

1. In Document360, go to **Settings → Integrations → HubSpot**.
2. Click **Connect HubSpot**.
3. Log in to your HubSpot account and authorize the connection.
4. Map your Document360 project to the relevant HubSpot portal.
5. Configure sync preferences.
6. Click **Save**.

---

## Features

- **Article Embedding** – Embed knowledge base articles in HubSpot emails and landing pages.
- **Contact Activity Tracking** – See which articles a contact has viewed in their CRM timeline.
- **Chatbot Integration** – Surface knowledge base articles in HubSpot chatbot conversations.

---

## Use Cases

- Reduce onboarding friction by sending new customers targeted documentation links.
- Track which docs prospects view before converting.
- Automate follow-up emails when contacts read specific articles.

---

## Best Practices

- Tag articles by customer segment to deliver personalized content.
- Use HubSpot workflows to trigger doc-based nurture sequences.
- Review contact-level doc engagement data to prioritize content improvements.

---

## Summary

The HubSpot integration bridges documentation with marketing and sales, enabling smarter customer engagement through relevant knowledge base content.
