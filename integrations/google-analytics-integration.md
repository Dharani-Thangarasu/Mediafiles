# Google Analytics Integration

Connect Document360 with Google Analytics to gain deep insights into how users interact with your documentation.

---

## Overview

Google Analytics provides detailed metrics about page views, session duration, bounce rates, and user demographics for your knowledge base.

---

## Prerequisites

- A Google Analytics account (GA4 recommended)
- Your **Measurement ID** (format: `G-XXXXXXXXXX`)
- Admin access to your Document360 project

---

## Setup Steps

1. Log in to your Document360 project.
2. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Google Analytics**.
3. Toggle the **Enable** switch.
4. Enter your **Measurement ID**.
5. Click **Save**.

Google Analytics tracking will be active on your documentation site immediately.

---

## Metrics You Can Track

- **Page Views** – Most visited articles
- **Session Duration** – Time spent on articles
- **Bounce Rate** – Users who leave without interaction
- **Traffic Sources** – Where visitors come from
- **Device & Location** – User device and geographic data

---

## Viewing Reports

1. Open [analytics.google.com](https://analytics.google.com).
2. Select your property.
3. Navigate to **Reports → Engagement → Pages and Screens** to see article performance.

---

## Best Practices

- Set up custom events for search queries and feedback clicks.
- Use GA4 audiences to identify high-value documentation readers.
- Review reports weekly to identify content gaps.

---

## Summary

The Google Analytics integration gives you powerful data to continuously improve your documentation based on real user behavior.
