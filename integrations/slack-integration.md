# Slack Integration

Integrate Document360 with Slack to receive real-time notifications about documentation updates directly in your Slack channels.

---

## Overview

The Slack integration allows your team to stay informed about changes in your knowledge base without leaving their primary communication tool.

---

## Prerequisites

- A Slack workspace with admin permissions
- A Document360 project with admin access

---

## Setup Steps

1. Navigate to **Settings → Integrations → Slack** in your Document360 dashboard.
2. Click **Connect with Slack**.
3. Authorize Document360 to access your Slack workspace.
4. Choose the Slack channel where notifications should be sent.
5. Configure which events trigger notifications (e.g., article published, article updated).
6. Click **Save**.

---

## Supported Notifications

- Article published
- Article updated
- Article deleted
- New comment added
- Team member joined

---

## Best Practices

- Use a dedicated `#docs-updates` channel to avoid noise in general channels.
- Fine-tune notification triggers to keep messages relevant.

---

## Summary

The Slack integration keeps your team aligned on documentation changes with minimal effort.
