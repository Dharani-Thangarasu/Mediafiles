# Microsoft Teams Integration

Connect Document360 with Microsoft Teams to receive documentation update notifications in your Teams channels.

---

## Overview

The Microsoft Teams integration ensures your team is always notified about important documentation changes, directly within Teams.

---

## Prerequisites

- Microsoft Teams with admin or channel permissions
- Document360 project admin access

---

## Setup Steps

1. In Document360, navigate to **Settings → Integrations → Microsoft Teams**.
2. Click **Connect to Teams**.
3. Sign in with your Microsoft account and authorize the connection.
4. Select the Teams channel for notifications.
5. Choose the events you want to track.
6. Click **Save**.

---

## Supported Events

- Article published or updated
- Category created or deleted
- New team member added
- Feedback received on an article

---

## Using the Teams Bot

Once connected, you can also use the Document360 bot in Teams to:

- Search articles by keyword
- Get quick links to specific documentation
- Receive summaries of recent changes

---

## Best Practices

- Use a `#documentation` channel to centralize all doc-related notifications.
- Configure alerts only for high-priority events to reduce noise.

---

## Summary

The Microsoft Teams integration helps documentation teams stay coordinated and informed without switching between applications.
